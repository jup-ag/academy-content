## Earning Rewards with Jupiter Rewards Hub

Rewards and incentives are the heartbeat of the vast majority of Web3 communities and blockchain apps. They're what keeps us coming back, staying active, and going deeper into the protocols we use and enjoy everyday. At Jupiter, we know you like rewards, and so do we! We built a place where every trade and every action within the Jupiverse ecosystem can earn you something back.

Welcome to the Jupiter Rewards Hub.

Jupiter Rewards Hub is a newly added, unified dashboard inside Jupiter where users can discover active rewards and partner campaigns, track their earnings, and claim rewards, all without leaving Jupiter platform. You can access it at any time via the "Rewards" button in the top navigation bar.

### What You'll See in the Hub

After connecting your wallet, the Rewards Hub shows two key numbers at the top right:

- **Total Earned**: the cumulative rewards you've earned across all campaigns
- **Claimable**: the amount currently available for you to claim

Below you can find a "Campaigns" section, where all active, upcoming, and past reward programs are listed. You can filter by status and search by campaign name.

### Types of Campaigns

**1. Active Staking Rewards (ASR)**

ASR is Jupiter's core staking reward program. Every quarter, a pool of JUP tokens is distributed to stakers who actively participate in governance, voting on proposals and contributing to the Jupiverse.

- The pool is 50M JUP per quarter
- Your allocation is calculated using a time-weighted average of your stake during the period
- You need an average of at least 50 JUP staked during the quarter to be eligible
- After each quarterly campaign ends, there is a 3-month window to claim your rewards

ASR is designed to reward our most active cadets for participating and contributing. The more engaged you are, the higher your voting power, and the larger your share of the pool. You can learn more about ASR in the [Jupiter Governance](/lessons/jupiter-governance) lesson.

**2. Partner Campaigns**

These campaigns are run in partnership with ecosystem projects. The model varies from partner to partner, but it often revolves around trading a specific token or engaging with a specific DeFi protocol during the campaign period, and standing a chance to win from a prize pool. You may also be able to refer people to join the campaigns, in order to earn a portion of their trading activity.

Past examples include TCG Rewards Season 1 ($1M in USDC) and TCG Rewards Season 2 ($2M in JupUSD). Prize pools and participation criterias range widely, so be sure to read through the campaign's terms and conditions carefully.

**3. JupLend Campaigns**

Some campaigns reward you for depositing specific assets on Jupiter Lend. For example, the JupLend x xStocks Campaign rewarded users who deposited xStocks assets to Jupiter Lend, with a share of a $20K JupUSD pool. These campaigns are a way to earn additional rewards on top of standard lending yield returns.

**4. Prediction Market Campaigns**

Jupiter Predict also runs its own reward campaigns. For example, the "100% Freeroll Prediction Raffle" campaign included a $50K prize pool to first-time predictors, if you placed a losing prediction of $500 or less, you still had a chance to win a full refund of the prediction amount.

**5. Mystery Rewards**

Occasionally, Jupiter and its partners distribute surprise rewards to eligible wallets. To check if you have one, import your wallet into the Jupiter Mobile app or browser extension, the hub will flag any unclaimed mystery rewards automatically.

### The Referral Program

The Rewards Hub has a dedicated referral tab. When someone signs up and joins a campaign using your referral link, you earn a percentage of their trading points, passively, on every trade they make. Referral fee rewards compounds over time as your referral network grows.

### Tips for Maximizing Your Rewards

1. Stake and vote every quarter
2. Check the Rewards Hub regularly, new campaigns launch frequently, and some have short windows
3. Don't forget to claim, rewards DO NOT auto-deposit, you need to manually claim within the window period.
4. Import your wallet to the Jupiter mobile app or extension, to check mystery rewards you might not know about yet.

### How to Claim Your Rewards

1. Go to [jup.ag/rewards](https://jup.ag/rewards) and connect your wallet
2. Browse the "Campaigns" section. Campaigns with available rewards will show a "Claim" button
3. Each campaign displays its claim window, make sure you claim before the deadline, or your rewards expire
4. Claimed rewards land directly in your wallet

> **Note:** the claim period is separate from the campaign period. For ASR, the campaign runs for 3 months, and then you have another 3 months to claim. Missing the claim window means forfeiting your rewards.

For the most up-to-date list of active campaigns, visit [https://jup.ag/rewards](https://jup.ag/rewards).
