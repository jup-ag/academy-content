## Why use a non-custodial wallet?

Non-custodial wallets are essential if you want to:

- Interact with Solana dApps such as Jupiter, Tensor, or Magic Eden  
- Participate in airdrops and on-chain activities  
- Maintain sovereignty over your assets  
- Avoid risks associated with centralized exchanges

When you create a wallet, you generate a **seed phrase** ‚Äî the master key to your crypto. Lose it and your funds are gone. Share it and your funds are gone even faster.

### Install the wallet extension

1. Go to the official Phantom website  
2. Choose your browser (Chrome, Brave, Firefox, Edge)  
3. Click **Add to Browser**  
4. Once installed, click the Phantom icon to open the setup screen

### Create your wallet

When the extension opens:

1. Click **Create New Wallet**  
2. Phantom will display your **12-word seed phrase**  
3. Write it down **on paper**, not on your phone or computer  
4. Store copies securely in two separate physical locations  
5. Set a strong password to lock the extension  

Your seed phrase is the only way to recover your wallet. Treat it like gold.

### Fund your wallet

To start using your wallet, you‚Äôll need a small amount of SOL to pay transaction fees.

You can acquire SOL by:

- Buying on a centralized exchange (Binance, Coinbase, etc.) and withdrawing to your new wallet  
- Receiving SOL from a friend  
- Using a non-KYC on-ramp (MoonPay, Transak, etc.)  

Once the SOL arrives, your wallet is officially live.
