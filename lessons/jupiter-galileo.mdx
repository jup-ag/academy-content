## Commanding Jupiter with Galileo

For years, whether onchain or in traditional finance, most software requires you to learn its language first. You study the menus, locate the right interface, configure the right parameters before you can use the tool to its maximum potential.

Jupiter Galileo changes that. It is the first AI-native layer built directly into the Jupiter suite of products. Instead of you learning the cryptographic language of Jupiter, Jupiter Galileo learns to understand you.

No menus to navigate, no settings to fiddle around in. You type what you want, and Galileo will figure out how to execute it.

> **Note:** Jupiter Galileo is in early access beta. It is actively being tested and refined, and some actions may have temporary restrictions. Always verify details before signing.

### What is Galileo?

Galileo is a Natural Language Interface, a type of software that accepts human language as input rather than structured commands or interface clicks. You type an instruction the same way you might describe it to a colleague, and the system interprets and executes it.

It's the total opposite of Jupiter Command Line Interface (CLI). Jupiter CLI provides you with raw ingredients to cook with - it is precise and powerful, but it requires you to know exactly how to phrase the instruction. On the other hand, Jupiter Galileo takes your order and serves the finished dish. You describe the outcome you want; Galileo handles the preparation.

It is worth being clear about what Galileo is not. It is not a general-purpose chatbot or a research assistant. It is a **command layer for action** - designed for users who know what they want to do and want to do it immediately, without navigating menus or switching between products.

### How Does Galileo Work?

When you submit an instruction, Galileo routes it through Jupiter's infrastructure to find the optimal path for execution. Under the hood, this means your command is resolved across multiple liquidity pools and decentralized exchanges - the same infrastructure that powers Jupiter Swap, Earn, Borrow, and the rest of the ecosystem - without you needing to touch any of those interfaces directly.

Galileo executes actions across Jupiter's core products, including:

- **Swaps**: Instant, limit, recurring
- **Staking**: Native SOL staking, JupSOL, JUP staking for governance
- **Lending**: Deposit and withdraw on Jupiter Lend
- **Position management**: View your portfolio, check balances, claim rewards

Galileo is also capable of **executing advanced workflows that span multiple products**. A single instruction can trigger a sequence of actions across different parts of Jupiter.

Jupiter Galileo's edge is the ability to remove friction and make Jupiter's entire ecosystem more accessible to anyone who can describe what they want. This truly marks the beginning of AI integration into Jupiter core products.

### Associated Risks with Galileo

The primary risk with any natural language interface is **misinterpretation**. Because Galileo translates your words into executable onchain transactions, users have to be sufficiently clear in their instructions to avoid risks of performing unwanted actions. Some risks might include:

- **Ambiguous phrasing produces different outcomes.** "Swap half my SOL" and "swap 5 SOL" are interpreted differently. Be explicit about amounts, tokens, and direction whenever possible.
- **Multi-step workflows compound risk.** A single instruction that triggers swaps across multiple products is higher-risk than a single swap, because a misinterpretation early in the chain affects every downstream step.
- **The confirmation step is your safety check.** Galileo will show you exactly what it has interpreted before any transaction is signed. Read it carefully - especially the asset, the direction, and the amount - and never approve a transaction you don't fully understand.

We recommend always double-checking your instruction before signing the transaction. Galileo will show you exactly what it has interpreted from your instruction - treat this confirmation step as your safety check.

### Using Jupiter Galileo

**Step 1: Open Galileo.**

Navigate to [jup.ag](https://jup.ag). In the bottom-right corner of the screen, you will see the Jupiter logo. Click it to open the Galileo panel.

![Galileo Button](https://static.academy.jup.ag/images/illustrations/galileo_button.png)

**Step 2: Type in Your Instructions.**

Once open, Galileo displays your net worth and current asset holdings at a glance. Enter your instruction in plain language. Some examples:

- Swap half my SOL to USDC
- Sell all my tokens and buy JupUSD
- Stake 10 SOL

Be as specific as you can. The clearer your instruction, the more accurately Galileo will interpret it.

![Galileo Homepage](https://static.academy.jup.ag/images/illustrations/galileo_homepage.png)

**Step 3: Review the Interpreted Action.**

Galileo will interpret your instruction and provide a summary of its intended actions. Read this carefully: verify the assets, amounts, and actions before proceeding.

**Step 4: Approve and Execute.**

Confirm the details and approve the transaction in your wallet. Galileo will then execute the action through Jupiter's infrastructure!
